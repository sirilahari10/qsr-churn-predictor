Risk Tier Distribution
Visit frequency drop is the single strongest churn signal — a >40% decline in visits between 90-day windows raises churn probability by ~25 points.
App disengagement precedes churn by 3–4 weeks on average. Zero app opens in 30 days is a reliable early warning signal.
Offer redemption is protective — customers who redeem at least 2 offers per quarter churn at roughly half the rate of non-redeemers.
